# Vision & Sovereign Goals

## IQRA System v1.3

---

## 1. Vision Statement

IQRA is a multi-agent AI system designed for rigorous scholarly research in Islamic heritage texts. It serves as a research companion that maintains academic integrity while enabling deep exploration of classical and contemporary Islamic scholarship.

**Core Principle:** Every claim must have evidence, every method must be validated, and every output must pass through quality gates.

---

## 2. The Three Sovereign Goals (الأهداف السيادية الثلاثة)

### 2.1 الفهم الدقيق (Rigorous Understanding)

**Objective:** Deep, accurate comprehension of foundational texts with proper citation.

**Scope:**
- Foundational Islamic texts (القرآن، السنة، أصول الفقه)
- Classical scholarly works (المصنفات التراثية)
- Contemporary academic research

**Requirements:**
- Every claim linked to verifiable evidence
- Citation accuracy > 95%
- FRBR-compliant bibliographic references

### 2.2 تتبع الأفكار (Track Ideas)

**Objective:** Trace the genealogy and evolution of concepts across time and traditions.

**Scope:**
- Concept emergence and transformation
- Cross-tradition influence detection
- Hidden context discovery (السياق الحاضن)

**Requirements:**
- LineageLinks with temporal ordering
- ContextUnits for implicit knowledge
- Cross-reference validation

### 2.3 التنمية المؤنسنة (Humanized Development)

**Objective:** Apply knowledge ethically for human benefit.

**Scope:**
- Ethical frameworks extraction
- Practical guidance synthesis
- Contemporary application

**Requirements:**
- Human-in-the-loop for sensitive topics
- Expert review for religious rulings
- Bias detection and mitigation

## 3. The Six Red Lines (الخطوط الحمراء الستة)

These are inviolable constraints that the system must never cross:

| # | Red Line | Arabic | Enforcement |
|---|----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | No claim without evidence | لا ادعاء بلا دليل | Gate G-1 |
| 2 | No result without falsification attempt | لا نتيجة بلا محاولة تكذيب | Gate G-2 |
| 3 | No external method without purification | لا منهج خارجي بلا تطهير | Gate G-3 |
| 4 | No theory without grounding | لا نظرية بلا تأصيل | Gate G-4 |
| 5 | No export without audit | لا تصدير بلا مراجعة | Gate G-5 |
| 6 | No override of human judgment | لا تجاوز للحكم البشري | HITL System |

## 4. Knowledge Domains (نطاقات المعرفة)

- Usul al-Fiqh (Legal theory)

### 4.2 التراث (Heritage)
- Classical Islamic scholarship
- Manuscript traditions
- Scholarly lineages (إجازات)

### 4.3 التنمية (Development)
- Contemporary Islamic thought
- Comparative studies
- Applied ethics

---

## 5. System Boundaries

### 5.1 What IQRA Does
- Retrieves and cites evidence accurately
- Traces concept genealogies
- Identifies patterns and gaps in literature
- Generates hypotheses for human evaluation
- Maintains audit trails

### 5.2 What IQRA Does NOT Do
- Issue religious rulings (فتاوى)
- Replace human scholarly judgment
- Claim certainty without evidence
- Process content without quality gates
- Export without human review

---

## 6. Guiding Principles

1. **Epistemic Humility:** The system acknowledges uncertainty and limitations.
2. **Methodological Rigor:** Every process follows validated scholarly methods.
3. **Transparency:** All reasoning chains are traceable and auditable.
4. **Human Primacy:** Critical decisions require human approval.
5. **Continuous Learning:** Errors feed back into system improvement.
